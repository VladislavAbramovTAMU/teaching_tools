IS–LM Model

The IS–LM model describes short-run equilibrium in the goods and money markets. The downward-sloping IS curve shows combinations of output Y and interest rate r that clear the goods market (investment equals saving). The upward-sloping LM curve shows combinations that clear the money market (money demand equals real money supply M/P). Their intersection determines equilibrium Y* and r*. Use the sliders to run fiscal policy experiments (shift IS via G or t) and monetary policy experiments (shift LM via M/P). Dashed curves show the baseline for comparison.
